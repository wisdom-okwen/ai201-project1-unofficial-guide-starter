Topic: Dorm packing essentials (with reasoning)
University: University of Texas at Austin

=== Essential Items ===
- Mattress topper: significantly improves comfort on the standard dorm-provided mattress; widely available during summer moving season.
- Quality pillow: recommended for better sleep. A body pillow also "creates a barrier" and prevents rolling off the bed; affordable at retailers like Target.
- Desk lamp: dorm overhead lighting is inadequate for studying; a desk lamp provides better illumination. Check your housing manual for compliance.
- Shower caddy and shower shoes: necessities for communal bathrooms. A mesh or plastic caddy transports toiletries easily; shower shoes protect your feet in shared showers.
- Water boiler: simplifies preparing hot water for ramen, tea, etc. — more convenient than microwaving water.

=== Enjoyable Additions ===
- Coffee machine: a Keurig or standard coffee maker saves time and money versus cafe visits and enables late-night brewing.
- Headphones or earbuds: essential for respecting roommate privacy while watching shows, attending online lectures, or commuting.
- Personal mementos: photos and posters provide comfort and connection to home.
