Source: Mount Bonnell / Longhorn Life — "10 Best Off-Campus Study Spots in Austin for UT Students" (unofficial off-campus housing blog)
URL: https://www.mountbonnell.info/longhorn-life/10-best-places-to-study-off-campus-in-austin
Topic: Off-campus study spots
University: University of Texas at Austin

1. Bennu Coffee (North Austin and Congress Avenue) — open 24 hours a day; spacious seating with numerous power outlets, indoor/outdoor options, fair-trade coffee, reliable WiFi, and large tables. Ideal for late-night study sessions.
2. Caffe Medici (Downtown Austin) — fast WiFi, friendly staff, natural light, comfortable seating, highly regarded coffee, quiet atmosphere.
3. Halcyon (Downtown Austin) — couches and communal tables, power outlets, diverse menu from coffee to cocktails, natural light.
4. The Drag (North Guadalupe Street, the western border of UT campus) — coffee shops and casual eateries with seating and WiFi, bookstores, vibrant street atmosphere.
5. The Library Coffeehouse — comfortable seating, natural light, quiet atmosphere, free WiFi, many power outlets, near public transit.
6. Cherrywood Coffeehouse — espresso bar and 25 craft beers, indoor/outdoor patio, pet-friendly, live music, vegetarian/vegan options.
7. Flightpath Coffeehouse — quiet, productive environment popular with grad students and coders; ample tables, reliable WiFi, plenty of outlets.
8. Pacha Austin (Rosedale) — cozy, large windows with natural light, tables and couches, high-quality coffee and homemade pastries, outdoor seating.
9. Sa-Ten Coffee & Eats — Japanese-inspired decor, spacious well-lit interior, power outlets, curry bowls and specialty toast, quiet ambiance.
10. Mozart's Coffee Roasters (3825 Lake Austin Blvd) — European-style cafe with stunning Lake Austin views, tree-shaded deck, specialty coffees and house-made pastries, weekend live music.
