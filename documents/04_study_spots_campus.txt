Source: Rambler ATX — "9 Best Study Spots Near UT Austin (Updated for 2026)" (unofficial off-campus housing blog)
URL: https://www.rambleratx.com/resources/best-study-spots-ut-austin/
Topic: Study spots (on and off campus)
University: University of Texas at Austin

=== On Campus ===
1. The Texas Union (2308 Whitis Ave) — known as the "living room" of campus; diverse lounge areas, patios, and meeting rooms. Dining options including Panda Express and Starbucks make it good for long sessions.
2. Life Science Library (110 Inner Campus Drive) — in the historic Main Building; exposed wood furnishings and a quiet environment. Empty seminar classrooms nearby offer private study options.
3. Engineering Education and Research Center / EER (2501 Speedway) — despite the name, students of all majors study here; open atrium and award-winning modern design.

=== Off Campus ===
4. Civil Goat Coffee (3423 Guadalupe St) — Austin-native coffee shop opened on Guadalupe in 2020; spacious indoor and outdoor seating, quality beverages, pastries.
5. Austin Central Library (710 W Cesar Chavez St) — six-story modern facility with workspaces and a rooftop garden.
6. Medici Roasting (2222 Guadalupe St B) — directly on Guadalupe, appeals to West Campus residents; known for quality coffee and breakfast tacos.
7. Mozart's Coffee Roasters (3825 Lake Austin Blvd) — on Lake Austin; scenic outdoor study destination with ample parking and an extensive pastry selection.
8. Summer Moon Coffee (3115 S 1st St) — the original location; wood-fired coffee and outdoor seating.
9. Lola Savannah Coffee Lounge (6317 Bee Cave Rd, Westlake) — open until 11pm daily; spacious, good for late-night studying.
