Topic: Dining and meal plans
University: University of Texas at Austin

=== Payment Methods ===
- Dine In Dollars: usable at campus restaurants, coffee shops, and convenience stores. Fall balances roll over to Spring but expire at the end of the year.
- Bevo Pay: works at University Housing and Dining locations plus participating off-campus merchants. Unlike Dine In Dollars, these funds carry over annually and are refundable upon graduation.

=== Residence Hall Plans ===
- Students in on-campus dorms automatically receive the Unlimited Meal Plan (included in room and board): unlimited dining-hall access at J2, Jester City Limits, and Kins Dining, plus $300 Dine In Dollars and $100 Bevo Pay per term.
- The optional Flex Meal Plan costs $500 per semester and replaces unlimited meals with $1,750 Dine In Dollars and $100 Bevo Pay per term.

=== Dobie Twenty21 Plans ===
- Longhorn 25: 25 meals + $25 Dine In Dollars ($325/semester)
- Longhorn 60: 60 meals + $60 Dine In Dollars ($600/semester)
- Dobie Unlimited: unlimited meals + $300 Dine In Dollars ($2,250/term)
- Starting Fall 2025, Dobie residents receive the on-campus Unlimited Plan included with housing costs.

=== Commuter Plans (Off-Campus Students) ===
- Longhorn 25: 25 meals + $25 Dine In Dollars ($325/semester)
- Longhorn 60: 60 meals + $60 Dine In Dollars ($600/semester)
- Longhorn Unlimited: unlimited meals + $100 Dine In Dollars ($1,650/semester)
