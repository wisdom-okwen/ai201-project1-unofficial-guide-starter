Topic: Dorm packing list / move-in
University: University of Texas at Austin

=== Room Essentials ===
Bedding for extra-long twin beds (36" x 80"), pillows, mattress pad, comforter, desk lamp (non-halogen), towels, shower caddy, TV, alarm clock, fan, computer, printer, mini-vacuum, wall mirror, trash bags, and toiletries.

=== Decorative Items ===
Photos, picture frames, photo clips, jewelry organizer, baskets, string lights, posters, and wall art.

=== Academic Supplies ===
Pencil sharpener, erasers, highlighters, ruler, scissors, external storage, wireless mouse, chargers, staplers, correction fluid, textbooks, iClicker (if required), calculator (verify the required model), laptop, backpack, pens, binders, paper, tote bag, calendar, folders, and sticky notes.

=== Bathroom & Hygiene ===
Toilet paper, body wash, shampoo, lotion, toothbrush, toothpaste, bathroom rug, shower shoes, shower cap, and towels.

=== Important Documents ===
ID, bank documents, credit/debit cards, checks, and health insurance cards.

=== Prohibited Items ===
Strictly forbidden: pets (except fish in 5-gallon-or-smaller tanks), certain lighting (halogens, LED string lights, neon), fireworks, weapons, candles, extension cords without circuit breakers, personal toilet seats, hazardous materials, grills, personal mattresses, homemade furniture, self-built lofts, multi-port USB chargers, wireless routers, 3D printers, and most cooking appliances (toasters, air fryers, hot plates, etc.).
